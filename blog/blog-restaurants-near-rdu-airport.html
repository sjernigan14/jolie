The Right Dinner After Landing at RDU

You've just landed at Raleigh-Durham International Airport. You have a client dinner, a team meal, or you're meeting family for the first time in months. You don't want airport food or a chain restaurant. You want something real. Crawford Brothers Steakhouse is 12 minutes from RDU via I-40. It's worth the drive.

What You Get

Chef Scott Crawford is a James Beard semifinalist. The restaurant runs its own dry-aging program—you can watch the meat cure through glass walls while you drink. The wine list runs over 300 bottles. Cocktails are handled by Beverage Director Jordan Joseph, who knows what he's doing. This is not a casual spot. This is the dinner you book because the work trip matters, because your clients deserve it, because you actually want to be there.

For Business Travelers and Layovers

If you're flying into Raleigh for a meeting, a conference, or a quick turnaround, Crawford Brothers handles it. The location is easy—take I-40 directly from the airport, no backtracking. Open until 10pm every night. Same-day reservations often available on weeknights through Tock. The private dining room seats up to 24. Book it for client entertainment, team dinners, or investor meetings.

Parking and Logistics

You're not hunting for street parking or dealing with valet chaos. The restaurant is in Fenton, a commercial development with parking. You drive, you park, you walk in. From terminal to table in under 30 minutes.

The Wine List and Drinks

Over 300 bottles. The cocktail menu is straightforward: classic drinks made properly. Jordan Joseph's approach is clarity, not flash. Bourbon, rye, gin, vermouth. The fundamentals done right.

Already Inside the Terminal? Crawford's Genuine

If your schedule won't allow the 12-minute drive, Chef Scott Crawford also operates Crawford's Genuine inside RDU Airport. Same culinary standards, built for the pace of air travel. It's the only Chef Scott Crawford concept you can walk to from your gate. But if you have the time—and you should make the time—Crawford Brothers is the full experience.
