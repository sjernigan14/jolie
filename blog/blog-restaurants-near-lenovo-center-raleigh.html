Where to Eat Near Lenovo Center

Whether you're catching a Carolina Hurricanes game, attending an NC State basketball matchup, or heading to a concert at Lenovo Center in Raleigh, the question of where to eat before or after the event matters. You want somewhere close enough to not add an hour to your evening, somewhere with quality food that feels like a real restaurant—not a chain. Crawford Brothers Steakhouse delivers on both counts. We're 9 minutes from Lenovo Center via I-40, located in Fenton, Cary's walkable dining district.

The 9-Minute Drive

Nine minutes isn't casual. It's real. You can leave home, park, order a cocktail, and have a knife in your hand before most pre-game traffic clears the parking garage. From Lenovo Center, take I-40 west toward Chapel Hill. Exit at Fenton Village/Cary. You're there. After the game—when everyone's heading back toward downtown—the same route runs in reverse. No waiting. No congestion.

Open When You Need Us

Crawford Brothers opens daily at 5pm and runs until 10pm. That covers every Hurricanes faceoff, every concert start time, every post-game reservation window. We're specifically built for this schedule. You won't show up and find us closing down. Dinner before the arena. Celebration after. Whatever the evening calls for.

What We Cook

Dry-aged prime steaks. That's the foundation. Our in-house dry-aging program means what you eat has spent weeks in our coolers, concentrating flavor and tenderness. It's not what you'd call fancy—it's what you'd call worth the drive. We work with straightforward preparations: high heat, proper seasoning, the kind of simplicity that only works if your ingredient is actually exceptional. Beyond the steaks, a full bar stocks craft cocktails and a wine list that runs 300+ bottles deep. If you're the type to want something specific—or to let a bartender talk you through what's new—this is a functioning bar, not a grab-a-beer spot.

Group Events and Private Dining

Taking a group to the game? Our private dining room seats up to 24. Pre-game dinner for the office. Post-game team celebration. Friends gathering before the Hurricanes season opener. It's booked through Tock like the rest of the restaurant—same approach, same kitchen, dedicated space for your party.

Book Your Reservation

Reservations are handled through Tock. Search Crawford Brothers Cary, pick your time, done. Make it early enough that you're not rushed. The pace of the meal should feel unhurried, even if the arena's starting to fill up.
