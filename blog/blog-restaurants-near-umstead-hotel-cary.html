Prime Steakhouse Dining Near The Umstead

December 2025

The Steakhouse Your Umstead Stay Deserves

You're staying at The Umstead Hotel and Spa—the only Five-Star AAA Five Diamond hotel in the Triangle. The concierge recommended three restaurants on-property. They're competent. But you came to Raleigh for the prime steakhouse experience, and nine minutes away, that's exactly what you'll find at Crawford Brothers. Located in the Fenton district just south of downtown, Crawford Brothers offers the kind of steakhouse dinner that pairs naturally with your Umstead stay. No resort pricing. No tired hospitality-hotel formality. Just Chef Scott Crawford's kitchen, dry-aged prime beef visible through glass, and a wine list that rewards serious attention.

What Sets Crawford Brothers Apart

The in-house dry-aging program is the centerpiece. Unlike most steakhouses that outsource aging and receive cuts prepped and wrapped, Crawford Brothers ages whole primals on-site. Glass walls run the length of the dining room—you can see the carcasses aging, understand the craft, and watch the kitchen work. This transparency extends to everything else: sourcing matters, technique matters, and presentation matters. Chef Scott Crawford is a James Beard semifinalist. His culinary ambition doesn't manifest in needless complexity—it appears in a perfectly seared scallop, a properly rendered prime steak, and the discipline to let premium ingredients define the plate. The seafood tower arrives regularly, stocked with oysters, shrimp, and crab. The wine program is overseen by Head Sommelier Mat Brochard, and the 300+ bottle list reflects genuine curation—not just depth, but thoughtfulness about what pairs with the menu.
