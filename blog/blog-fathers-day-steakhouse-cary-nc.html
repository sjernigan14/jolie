Father's Day at Crawford Brothers

September 2025

A Real Steakhouse for Father's Day

Father's Day calls for a real steakhouse—not a chain, not a compromise. Crawford Brothers in Fenton, Cary delivers exactly that. Prime dry-aged cuts under glass, bourbon that reads like a manifesto, and the kind of room that lets a father breathe. This is where to land.

The Steaks: Dry-Aged and Visible

Walk in and you see it immediately—our dry-aging program behind glass walls. Thirty-five to forty-five-day prime beef hanging in controlled conditions, darkening and concentrating. The steaks that come from that glass come aged exactly right—mineral, nutty, with a crust that takes a sear like it's supposed to. For Father's Day, order a dry-aged ribeye or bone-in strip. The difference between this and supermarket beef exists because this beef has had time to become itself—less water, more flavor.

The Bourbon Program

Beverage Director Jordan Joseph doesn't do volume. He does intention. The bourbon and whiskey program is built on cocktails that matter—the CB Old Fashioned is the template. The Port of Spain follows close. Both are Father's Day staples. The wine list runs over 300 bottles.

The Gangster Room

Six seats. Behind the dry-aging glass. The Gangster Room is intimate—the kind of space where a Father's Day dinner becomes something he remembers. You can request it for your reservation. The rest of the dining room runs open and airy—high ceilings, visible kitchen, bar sightlines.

What's a good Father's Day restaurant in Cary NC?

Crawford Brothers Steakhouse in Fenton, Cary is the definitive choice for Father's Day. Prime dry-aged steaks sourced from a visible in-house aging program, a bourbon and whiskey program curated by Beverage Director Jordan Joseph, and the intimate Gangster Room private space create an experience built for the occasion. The combination of serious cooking, intentional cocktails, and genuine hospitality makes Crawford Brothers a destination steakhouse worthy of celebration.

Does Crawford Brothers have a whiskey program?

Yes. Beverage Director Jordan Joseph curates bourbon and whiskey with intention—not quantity. The cocktail program centers on classics that matter: the CB Old Fashioned and Port of Spain are Father's Day staples, built with premium spirits and proper technique. The wine list spans over 300 bottles, giving you options at every price point and flavor preference to pair with your steak.
