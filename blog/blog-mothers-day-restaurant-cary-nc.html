Mother's Day Dinner at Crawford Brothers

Mother's Day calls for a restaurant that doesn't just serve good food—one that understands occasion. Crawford Brothers, situated at Fenton in Cary, delivers exactly that. The dry-aged prime steaks arrive prepared with precision. The wine list runs 300+ bottles deep. The service moves with intention. And the space—intimate enough for the moment, refined without pretense—makes it clear that thought went into every detail.

The Wine Program

Head Sommelier Mat Brochard and Assistant Sommelier Anita Tingle have built something that goes beyond a list. Twenty-five selections pour by the glass, ranging $16–$65, which means there's genuinely something for every budget and preference. The full list spans 300+ bottles. If your mother enjoys exploring wine, Brochard and Tingle offer real guidance—not shortcuts.

The Meal Itself

Dry-aged prime steaks form the backbone. The aging process concentrates flavor and changes texture—the meat becomes tender in a specific way that fresh beef cannot match. The seafood tower offers an alternative approach—oysters, shrimp, crab—all pointing toward brightness and the sea. Tableside experiences—Caesar salads built in front of you, steak cut and plated with ceremony—bring back something that modern dining often loses: presence.

The Setting

The main dining room works for two people or a small group. The private dining room—seating 24—handles larger family gatherings without feeling cavernous. You can book it for the whole party.

The Practical Details

Book through Tock at exploretock.com/crawford-brothers-cary. Mother's Day fills quickly—reserve well in advance. The restaurant sits at 401 Fenton Gateway Dr., Cary, with parking immediate. Service runs daily 5pm–10pm. Gift cards are available—call (919) 629-4029.
