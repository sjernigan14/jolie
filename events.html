Private Events

Celebrate with Us

Host Your Event at Jolie

Whether it's an intimate dinner, a milestone celebration, a corporate gathering, or a wedding rehearsal, Jolie offers two distinct spaces for your private event. From our candlelit rooftop terrace to our elegant main dining room, we create memorable experiences for our guests and yours. Our team handles the details — you just show up and celebrate.

Le Rooftop Buyout

Our rooftop terrace is available for private buyouts of up to 30 guests. Surrounded by lush greenery and candlelit tables beneath our custom-designed canopy, created by Louis Cherry Architecture, your guests will feel transported. Choose from our full French bistro menu, select wines and cocktails, and enjoy the evening with a dedicated service team that treats your event like their own.

Full Restaurant Buyout

For larger celebrations, Jolie's main dining room is available for full restaurant buyouts accommodating up to 50 guests. The warmth and intimacy of our bistro becomes the backdrop for your event — every detail from the lighting to the plating is thoughtfully executed. A customized menu, full bar service, and our entire team dedicated to your evening.

The Experience

What to Expect

When you book a private event at Jolie, you're not just renting a space — you're joining our family for an evening. Our events team works with you to understand your vision, customize a menu that reflects your taste and occasion, and handle logistics so you can focus on your guests. Whether you're planning a cocktail reception with passed hors d'oeuvres or a multi-course seated dinner, we craft every moment with intention and warmth.
