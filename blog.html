January 15, 2026

A Taste of Paris on Person Street

There's something magical about stepping through the doors of Jolie—a moment where the bustling energy of Historic Oakwood melts away, and you're transported to the warm, intimate embrace of a true French bistro. Named after Scott Crawford's beloved daughter, Jolie represents not just a restaurant, but a philosophy: the belief that exceptional food and genuine hospitality should be woven into the fabric of our community.

When you visit Jolie, you're stepping into a carefully crafted world of French bistro tradition. The menu celebrates classic dishes executed with precision—think steak frites with a perfect crust and yielding interior, escargot perfumed with garlic and parsley, and the kind of sauce work that takes years to master. But this isn't about nostalgia or self-conscious tradition; it's about honoring the fundamental techniques and flavors that make French bistro cuisine timeless, while infusing them with the energy and personality of modern Raleigh.

We're open Tuesday through Saturday from 5pm to 10pm, welcoming both reservations through Tock and walk-in guests based on availability. Our location at 620 N. Person Street puts us at the heart of Historic Oakwood, a neighborhood that embodies the same character and warmth we strive for in every plate we serve. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, enjoying a quiet dinner for two, or simply seeking an evening of beautiful food and thoughtful service, Jolie is here to make it memorable.

The story of Jolie is the story of Crawford Hospitality—a family committed to elevating dining culture in North Carolina through passion, precision, and an unwavering dedication to our guests and our community.

November 8, 2025

Dining Under the Stars on Le Rooftop

One of Raleigh's most coveted dining experiences awaits you above the streets of Historic Oakwood. Le Rooftop at Jolie offers something increasingly rare in our city: an authentic rooftop dining venue designed with meticulous attention to beauty and comfort. This isn't an afterthought or a seasonal pop-up; it's a permanent testament to our commitment to creating extraordinary spaces where guests can celebrate life's important moments.

Designed by the talented team at Louis Cherry Architecture, Le Rooftop transforms an ordinary evening into something extraordinary. A custom canopy provides weather protection without sacrificing the magic of dining under the open sky, while lush greenery and carefully curated landscaping create a garden-like oasis above the city. Candlelit tables cast a warm, romantic glow, and the thoughtful design ensures that each guest enjoys both privacy and a sense of connection to the energy around them. Whether it's a summer evening with a gentle breeze or a cooler autumn night, our team carefully manages weather considerations to ensure your comfort year-round.

Le Rooftop accommodates intimate dinners for two as well as larger celebrations. We welcome private buyouts for groups up to 30 guests, making it an exceptional choice for engagements, anniversaries, birthdays, and corporate events. The combination of our refined French bistro cuisine, an all-French wine list, and the undeniable romance of rooftop dining creates memories that linger long after the last course.

This is what rooftop dining in Raleigh should be: a destination that marries culinary excellence with stunning ambiance, proving that you don't need to travel to Paris to find that quintessential French experience.

French Bistro Classics Meet North Carolina

At the heart of Jolie's culinary philosophy lies a beautiful paradox: honoring the refined techniques and time-tested recipes of French bistro tradition while celebrating the exceptional ingredients that make North Carolina's food scene so compelling. Chef Scott Crawford has spent his career understanding that the best bistro food isn't precious or complicated—it's about taking the finest ingredients and treating them with respect, allowing their character to shine through masterful technique and thoughtful preparation.

Our farm-to-table approach means building relationships with local producers who share our commitment to quality. Seasonal vegetables arrive at our kitchen at peak ripeness, proteins are sourced from trusted suppliers who understand sustainable and humane practices, and every element on the plate reflects both our location and our values. When you order steak frites at Jolie, you're enjoying impeccably selected beef prepared with the classical technique that makes this humble dish a cornerstone of bistro culture worldwide. When you try our escargot, you taste garlic, parsley, and butter in perfect harmony—simplicity perfected through practice and precision.

But our menu isn't frozen in time. Alongside the classics that define bistro cuisine, Chef Crawford crafts seasonal dishes that celebrate what's best right now. Perhaps a composed salad with local lettuces and vegetables prepared in the French classical style, or an innovative preparation of North Carolina seafood that speaks to both tradition and the present moment. This balance—respecting tradition while staying alive to possibility—is what keeps our menu vital and exciting for guests who visit regularly.

We invite you to discover what happens when French bistro mastery meets the incredible bounty of North Carolina. Reserve your table and experience the philosophy that drives everything we create.

The Art of the French Wine List

A proper French bistro isn't complete without a wine program that understands the fundamental truth: wine exists to enhance the joy of food and company. At Jolie, our all-French wine list isn't a flex or a historical reenactment—it's a curated reflection of our philosophy about how wine and bistro cuisine belong together. Every bottle on our list has been selected because we believe it will elevate your meal and deepen your appreciation for the flavors in your glass.

Our wine program celebrates the breadth and complexity of French winemaking traditions. From elegant Burgundies and Bordeaux to characterful wines from lesser-known regions, from prestigious producers to emerging winemakers who are pushing the boundaries of what's possible in their terroir, our list tells the story of French wine's incredible diversity. We believe that some of the most memorable wine experiences come not from the most expensive bottles, but from discovering a wine that perfectly complements your meal at a price that feels right—a well-made Côtes du Rhône or Loire Valley white that sings alongside our bistro cuisine.

Beyond wine, our craft cocktail program applies the same philosophy of balance and intentionality. Our bartenders understand that a great cocktail is like a great dish—it's about harmony, quality ingredients, and technique. Whether you're enjoying a classic Negroni or a house creation that plays with traditional bistro flavors, each drink is prepared with the same care and precision that defines our kitchen.

We invite you to explore our beverage program not as an intimidating exercise, but as an extension of the pleasure that great food creates. Our team is here to guide recommendations, answer questions, and help you discover pairings that delight you. Come sit at our bar, reserve a table, and let wine and food remind you why the French have been perfecting this dance for centuries.

A Family Table: The Crawford Hospitality Story

Behind every great restaurant lies a story of vision, persistence, and a fundamental belief in the power of food to bring people together. The Crawford Hospitality family—which includes Jolie, Crawford & Son, Brodeto, Crawford Brothers Steakhouse, and Bar Sousterre—shares a common thread that ties each concept together: a commitment to culinary excellence, genuine hospitality, and a deep roots in community. This isn't a restaurant group built on formulas or franchises; it's a family's evolving conversation about what it means to feed people well.

Each Crawford restaurant expresses a different facet of this philosophy. Jolie celebrates the refinement and intimate joy of French bistro dining. Crawford & Son honors the craft of fine dining with seasonal creativity and technical mastery. Brodeto brings the vibrant energy and flavors of the Mediterranean to our table. Crawford Brothers Steakhouse represents the American tradition of a great steak and spirited hospitality. Bar Sousterre offers a sophisticated refuge for cocktails and conversation. Together, these five concepts create a dining landscape that reflects the breadth of culinary traditions and experiences that nourish us.

But what truly defines Crawford Hospitality is the human element. We are a family business, built by people who care deeply about the quality of every experience—from the moment a guest walks through the door to the lasting impression of a meal they'll remember years later. Our team members aren't anonymous workers; they're part of a community that values growth, learning, and the genuine relationships that form when people work together toward something meaningful. We invest in our people because we understand that hospitality flows from real care and attention.

This family table extends beyond our restaurants to the Raleigh community itself. We're committed to sourcing locally, supporting other businesses that share our values, and creating spaces where people from all walks of life can gather, celebrate, and connect. The Crawford Hospitality story is still being written—with each service, each guest, each meal, we're adding another chapter to what it means to be a family of restaurants dedicated to excellence and community. We invite you to pull up a chair and become part of our story.
