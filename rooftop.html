Le Rooftop

Rooftop Dining

One of Raleigh's Only Rooftop Dining Experiences

Le Rooftop at Jolie is unlike anything else in the Triangle. Just one flight up from the dining room, step into an open-air terrace where candlelit tables sit beneath a custom-designed canopy, lush greenery frames every corner, and the evening sky becomes your ceiling. Named among the Triangle's best rooftop bars by Axios and highlighted by Eater as a standout feature of the restaurant, Le Rooftop has become one of Raleigh's most sought-after dining experiences — and for good reason.

The Experience

Dining Under the Stars

Imagine this: a warm Raleigh evening, a table set with candlelight, the quiet hum of conversation around you, and the full Jolie menu at your fingertips. Le Rooftop was designed for exactly this kind of night — the kind where time moves a little slower and every course feels like it belongs. Our full French bistro menu is served nightly on the terrace, from steak frites to escargot to whatever the pastry team has dreamed up for dessert. The craft cocktail and wine list are the same you'd find downstairs, and the service carries the same warmth — just with a breeze and a view of Historic Oakwood.

Architecture & Design

Designed by Louis Cherry Architecture

Le Rooftop was designed in collaboration with Louis Cherry Architecture, the Raleigh-based firm known for thoughtful, place-specific design. The custom canopy structure provides shelter without enclosure — open enough to feel the air, covered enough to create an intimate atmosphere even on the busiest evenings. Lush greenery and climbing plants soften every edge, wrought-iron details nod to Parisian terraces, and the lighting was calibrated to feel like dusk all night long. The result is a romantic complement to our main dining room that is, quite simply, unlike any other in the area.

Private Buyouts

Le Rooftop is available for private buyouts — cocktail parties, seated dinners, celebrations, and corporate gatherings for up to 30 guests. Your party will have the entire terrace, the full bistro menu, and a dedicated service team. Buyouts can be tailored to your occasion, whether that's a prix fixe dinner, a cocktail reception, or something in between. For inquiries, contact Kat Rider directly.

Weather & Seasons

Le Rooftop is open seasonally, generally from spring through fall. Our custom canopy includes curtains that can be drawn for mild rain, keeping you comfortable through the occasional Carolina drizzle. In the event of severe weather, we'll reach out to reschedule your reservation so you can enjoy the full rooftop experience on a better evening. We want every night up here to feel special.
