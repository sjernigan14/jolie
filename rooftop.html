le rooftop

dine under the stars

the experience

As evening descends on Raleigh, you ascend a narrow staircase to discover a secret waiting above the city streets. Le Rooftop emerges as an enchanted garden suspended between earth and sky—one of the only rooftop dining experiences in Raleigh, and certainly the most distinctly Parisian. The moment you step into this intimate space, the bustling world below fades away, replaced by the gentle flicker of candlelight, the soft rustle of lush greenery, and the vast expanse of open air overhead.

The custom canopy designed by Louis Cherry Architecture creates the perfect balance: sheltered yet open, intimate yet expansive. Strings of warm lights trace the canopy's lines while candlelit tables beckon you to sit, linger, and lose yourself in conversation. The lush greenery creates a private garden in the sky, transforming this rooftop into a space that feels worlds away from the street below—as if you've stumbled upon a hidden terrasse in the 6th arrondissement rather than downtown Raleigh. This is the magic of Le Rooftop: it transports you. The full French bistro menu is available nightly, allowing you to enjoy the same refined cuisine and thoughtful wine pairings you'd experience in our dining room, but with the romance and freedom of dining beneath the stars. It's the kind of place that transforms ordinary evenings into unforgettable memories, where the candlelight catches in your glass, conversation flows easily, and time seems to slow down. Recently recognized by Axios as one of the Triangle's best rooftop bars, Le Rooftop has become the destination for those seeking something truly special—a romantic dinner for two, a celebration with friends, or simply an evening that feels distinctly, irreplaceably French.

the design

The design of Le Rooftop is the result of thoughtful collaboration between our vision and the architectural expertise of Louis Cherry Architecture. Every element was selected to create a space that feels both timeless and distinctly intimate. The custom canopy overhead provides shelter from the elements while maintaining the openness and airy quality essential to rooftop dining. Beneath it, the lush greenery creates the sensation of a private garden—verdant, alive, and designed to evolve with the seasons. The candlelit tables are arranged to encourage both romantic connection and easy social gathering, with each table positioned to maximize views of the Raleigh skyline while maintaining an enclosed, tranquil feeling. The careful curation of materials, lighting, and botanical elements transforms what could simply be functional outdoor space into something magical—a dining destination where design serves hospitality, where every detail contributes to the overall experience of feeling transported to another place entirely.

rain or shine

Le Rooftop is designed to welcome you in nearly all conditions. Our custom canopy and weather-resistant curtains keep you comfortable through light rain and cool breezes, ensuring that weather needn't interrupt your evening. In the rare case of severe weather, our team will work with you directly to reschedule your reservation for another evening—we want your experience to be as memorable and comfortable as possible.

Le Rooftop operates seasonally, with availability varying throughout the year. We recommend checking availability on our Tock reservation system or calling us at 919.803.7221 to confirm that your preferred date is open. Our team is always happy to help you secure the perfect evening on the rooftop.

private events

Le Rooftop is the perfect setting for your special celebration. Whether you're envisioning an intimate cocktail party, a private dinner with friends, or a milestone celebration, our rooftop can accommodate your vision. With capacity for up to 30 guests, Le Rooftop offers an exclusive, sophisticated setting that your guests will not forget. You'll have access to our full French bistro menu, allowing you to curate an experience that perfectly reflects your occasion and tastes.

To explore private event possibilities, contact Kat Rider, our events director, at kat@restaurantjolie.com or 919.803.7221. Kat will work with you to understand your vision, confirm available dates, discuss menu options, and create an evening that feels entirely yours.
