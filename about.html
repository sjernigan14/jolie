our story

our story
"After a family trip to Paris, we discovered our daughter Jolie's natural affinity for the French language, cuisine, and general joie de vivre, and instantly wanted to bring this quintessential Parisian dining experience to our community in a way that captures her joyful spirit."

In the heart of Historic Oakwood, just steps from our flagship Crawford & Son, we created Jolie as a love letter to Paris and everything our daughter discovered there. Jolie is not just a restaurant—it's an intimate neighborhood bistro where the warmth of French hospitality meets the genuine care of a family-driven establishment. When you walk through our doors, you're entering a space designed to feel like you've stumbled upon a cherished secret in the 6th arrondissement, transported to a quiet corner of Raleigh where time moves a little slower and conversation flows a little easier.

Every element of Jolie has been thoughtfully curated to honor authentic French bistro traditions while celebrating the refined artistry that defines our kitchen. Our menu features classic French dishes prepared with impeccable technique—coq au vin, duck confit, sole meunière—alongside thoughtfully conceived modern interpretations that reflect our commitment to seasonality and local sourcing. The wine program is intentionally curated to complement our food, offering both legendary French selections and carefully chosen alternatives that enhance the dining experience.

What truly sets Jolie apart is the atmosphere we cultivate every evening. It's the kind of place where regulars become friends, where first-time guests feel instantly welcomed, and where the distinction between server and family blurs in the most meaningful way. The candlelit tables, the gentle hum of conversation, the aroma of butter and fresh herbs—these are the details that remind us why we opened Jolie. We wanted to give our community a place where French hospitality and genuine warmth are not just promised but delivered with every course, every interaction, every evening.

the chef
Chef photo coming soon

Award-winning Chef Scott Crawford has spent his career blazing a trail across the South, earning Forbes Five-Star awards and five James Beard Foundation semifinalist nominations for "Best Chef: Southeast" before opening his flagship restaurant Crawford & Son—honored as "Best Restaurant of the Year." Crawford is known for his refined, artful cuisine, his thoughtful leadership both in and out of the kitchen, and his mission to celebrate his family through his work.

In 2019, inspired by his daughter's transformative love of Paris and all things French, Crawford opened Jolie—a French bistro that captures the intimate warmth and exceptional craft of the neighborhood bistros throughout the City of Light. The restaurant has quickly become a beloved destination in Historic Oakwood, earning recognition from critics and diners alike. In 2025, Jolie was selected by the MICHELIN Guide, a distinction that reflects Crawford's unwavering commitment to culinary excellence and the warm hospitality that defines the restaurant.

For Crawford, Jolie represents something deeply personal: the intersection of his culinary mastery and his family's joy. It's a space where his daughter's spirit—her exuberance, her refined palate, her infectious love of life—is woven into every aspect of the dining experience. When you dine at Jolie, you're not just experiencing his cooking; you're sharing in a family's love of Paris and, more importantly, their passion for bringing people together around exceptional food and genuine connection.
