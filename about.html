Our Story

The Beginning

A Taste of Paris on Person Street

After a family trip to Paris, we discovered our daughter Jolie's natural affinity for the French language, cuisine, and general joie de vivre — and instantly wanted to bring this quintessential Parisian dining experience to our community. The driving forces behind Jolie are happiness and simplicity. When guests walk in and look at the menu, see the design and feel the vibe, we want them to be instantly happy.

Jolie opened in August 2019 at 620 N. Person Street in Raleigh's Historic Oakwood neighborhood, right next to our sister restaurant Crawford & Son. Within hours of opening reservations, nearly 2,000 tables were booked — a testament to the community that has supported us from the beginning. For us, that sense of fellowship and community building is part of what dining out is about. It's why, when we open our restaurants, family is never far from mind.

Chef & Owner

Scott Crawford

Chef Scott Crawford has been credited with having a heavy hand in developing Raleigh's restaurant scene. He earned a Forbes Five-Star designation as executive chef at Herons — one of only seventy-nine in the country at the time — and has been named a James Beard Foundation semifinalist for Best Chef: Southeast five times. In 2016, he opened Crawford & Son, which was named Best Restaurant of the Year. Three years later, he opened Jolie, followed by Brodeto, Crawford Brothers Steakhouse, and Bar Sousterre.

Crawford's cooking at Jolie reflects the technique and joy behind classic French cuisine — refined and artful, but always warm. Steak frites au poivre, escargot, duck cassoulet, and a roast chicken that has earned its own following are hallmarks of a menu that is small, purposeful, and built on the belief that every dish should earn its place. The MICHELIN Guide recognized Jolie in its 2025 selection, a distinction that honors the team's continued commitment to culinary excellence.

General Manager

Kat Rider

Kat Rider oversees the dining experience at Jolie, ensuring that the warmth and attention to detail in the kitchen extend to every table, every guest, every evening. Under her leadership, the front-of-house team creates the kind of hospitality that makes first-time visitors feel like regulars — attentive without hovering, polished without pretense, and always rooted in genuine care. For private events and Le Rooftop inquiries, Kat is also your first point of contact.
