Join Our Team

At Jolie, we believe that great food comes from a passionate, dedicated team. We're a family-oriented restaurant that values collaboration, creativity, and a genuine love for French cuisine and hospitality. As part of the Crawford Hospitality family, you'll have the opportunity to grow your career alongside talented chefs, sommeliers, and hospitality professionals in one of Raleigh's most beloved dining destinations.

Whether you're an experienced chef looking to refine your craft, a server passionate about wine and French traditions, or someone eager to learn in a supportive kitchen environment, we'd love to hear from you. We offer competitive compensation, ongoing training, and the chance to be part of something truly special in Historic Oakwood.
