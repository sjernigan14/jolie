work with us

At Jolie, we believe that our team is the heart of everything we do. Working here means being part of a MICHELIN Guide Selected French bistro—a place where hospitality, culinary excellence, and community come together. As part of the Crawford Hospitality family, you'll be surrounded by passionate professionals who are committed to creating memorable experiences for our guests every single day.

We value individuals who take pride in their craft, whether you're in the kitchen, serving our guests, or welcoming them at the door. Our culture celebrates collaboration, continuous learning, and a genuine love for French bistro dining. We invest in our team's development and growth, offering opportunities to work alongside seasoned professionals while contributing your unique talents to our vibrant restaurant family. If you're ready to be part of something special—a place where excellence is the standard and hospitality is a way of life—we'd love to meet you.
